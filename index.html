    THE PHILIPPINE
    BLOOD BANK MANAGEMENT

    Blood bank has truly become the premier humanitarian organization in the cou, committed to provide quality life-saving services that protect the life and dignity especially of indigent Filipinos in vulnerable situations.

    The story of the Blood bank is the story of men and women from all walks of life who have dedicated themselves to the service of humanity. It is the tale of hundreds of thousands of ordinary people who devoted their time and resources to help the poorest of the poor. Professionally trained and truly compassionate, these men and women are ready to lend a helping hand to those in need â€“ whoever, whenever and wherever they may be.

    In all its 74 years of reputable existence, the Blood bank has lived through many changes that were intended to help more people. Where it used to be involved only in providing blood and in disaster-related activities, the Blood bank now focuses on a holistic approach to uplift the condition of the most vulnerable. Where it used to offer short-term palliatives, it now offers a wider array of humanitarian services ranging from preventive medicine to therapeutic counseling, to youth leadership.

    The Blood bank currently offers six key services: blood services, disaster management services, safety services, health services, social services, red cross youth programs, and volunteer services. Humanity, impartiality, neutrality, independence, voluntary service, unity, and universality are all essential ideals of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement. These ideals guide and motivate all Red Cross personnel and volunteers, for whom being a Red Crosser is a way of life rather than a philosophy.

    MISSION

    The Blood bank will provide a sustained and effective humanitarian service committed to building resilient communities, led by well-trained and dedicated staff and volunteers imbued with integrity, equipped with the necessary logistics, and utilizing information technology to the greatest extent possible. We will continue to develop our volunteer network around the cou to guarantee that our services are delivered as quickly as possible.

    VISION

    To be the leading humanitarian organization capable of responding quickly to difficulties and preventing and alleviating human suffering while elevating the dignity of the most vulnerable.
